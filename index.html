Our Story

Rooted in the Coast.
Expansive in Reach.

Rooted in Malindi on the Kenyan Coast, Crislynn Ventures is a boutique travel and lifestyle company dedicated to creating seamless, authentic, and unforgettable journeys. Our expertise along the coast gives us a unique perspective on the rhythms, colours, and experiences that make Kenya extraordinary.

From this foundation, we have grown to offer travel solutions across Kenya and beyond — blending premium mobility, handpicked accommodations, and immersive experiences to craft journeys that go far beyond the ordinary.

Our Experiences

Journeys That Stay With You

Curated coastal escapes, cultural encounters, island hideaways, and signature safaris — each professionally designed to balance discovery with comfort.

01

Coast & Islands

Salt air. Slow time. Endless horizons.

Full Day

Dhow Cruise Experience

Sail the Indian Ocean the timeless Swahili way. Swimming, sunset views, and moments the ocean keeps for you.

  • Afternoon pick-up and transfer to departure point
  • Welcome aboard followed by a relaxed coastal sail
  • Opportunities for swimming and photography
  • Spectacular sunset views across the water
  • Evening return transfer
Full Day

Island Escapades

Secluded shores, gentle tides, island time at its finest. Robinson or Sardenya Island — your choice.

  • Morning pick-up and transfer to departure point
  • Boat ride to Robinson or Sardenya Island
  • Time to swim, snorkel, beachcomb, or unwind
  • Fresh coastal lunch
  • Afternoon leisure before return transfer
Full Day

Watamu Marine Explorer

Coral gardens, turquoise waters, and the quiet rhythm of the ocean.

  • Morning pick-up and transfer to Watamu
  • Traditional dhow cruise across the marine park
  • Guided snorkeling with vibrant marine life
  • Explore scenic tidal creeks with birdwatching
  • Lunch at a renowned seafood restaurant
  • Optional kayaking or beach relaxation
02

Culture & Heritage

Stories written in stone, spice, and tradition.

Full Day

Ancient Stones to Mangrove Forest

From ancient ruins to whispering mangroves — a journey through time and nature.

  • Morning pick-up and scenic coastal drive
  • Guided tour of the ancient Gedi Ruins
  • Lunch at a nearby restaurant
  • Guided walk along the Mida Creek boardwalk
  • Learn about mangrove ecosystems and conservation
  • Return transfer
Half / Full Day

Swahili Bites & Henna Delight

Taste, create, and connect with the vibrant spirit of the Swahili coast.

  • Pick-up and introduction to Swahili culture
  • Guided visit to local markets
  • Henna artistry with optional hair braiding
  • Hands-on cooking session and tasting experience
  • Cultural storytelling and shopping time
  • Return transfer
Full Day

Golden Sands & Marafa Adventure

Sculpted canyons, golden dunes, and island calm — one unforgettable coastal journey.

  • Morning pick-up with brief tour orientation
  • Scenic drive to Marafa Canyon ("Hell's Kitchen")
  • Continue to the Mambrui Sand Dunes
  • Lunch at a beachside or local restaurant
  • Afternoon relaxation at Robinson Island
  • Late afternoon return transfer
03

Safaris

Where the land still leads.

Full Day

Tsavo East One-Day Safari

A single day. Africa's wilderness. Endless possibility.

  • Pick-up from Malindi; scenic drive to Tsavo East
  • Morning game drive through the park
  • Lunch at a safari lodge or tented camp
  • Afternoon game drive
  • Return to Malindi
3 Days · 2 Nights

Tsavo East & West Safari

Two legendary parks. One unforgettable safari.

  • Day 1: Pick-up from Malindi; Tsavo East game drive; overnight
  • Day 2: Morning game drive; transfer to Tsavo West; afternoon game drive; overnight
  • Day 3: Morning game drive; breakfast; return to Malindi
7 Days · 6 Nights

Bush to Beach — Maasai Mara & Malindi

From the drama of the savannah to the calm of the ocean.

  • Day 1: Arrival in Nairobi; transfer to hotel; overnight
  • Day 2: Transfer to Maasai Mara; afternoon game drive; overnight
  • Day 3: Full day Maasai Mara game drives; overnight
  • Day 4: Optional balloon safari; afternoon game drive; overnight
  • Day 5: Morning game drive; return to Nairobi
  • Day 6: Flight to Malindi; transfer to beach resort; overnight
  • Day 7: Beach leisure; transfer to airport
