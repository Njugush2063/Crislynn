Our Story

Rooted in Kenya.
Refined for you.

Crislynn Ventures was born from a simple belief: that travel should feel like coming home, even when you're somewhere entirely new. Our founders grew up along Kenya's coast, learning the tides, the cultures, and the quiet magic that most visitors never find.
